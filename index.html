    We at SHELPRINTS India Specialize in Decals

    SHELPRINTS India was started in 1994 as a joint venture with SHELPRINTS Holland. SHELPRINTS India's inception in India was in collaboration with K H Bailey, UK in order to maintain the quality and workmanship that SHELPRINTS is known to provide worldwide.
    We have set up a "State of Art" manufacturing facility in Bangalore, India. SHELPRINTS India today is an independent entity that conforms to European standards, retaining the benefit of competitive pricing, while also setting the industry standard in the country.
    SHELPRINTS India has set the standard for homegrown luxury in Decals. Our State of the Art facility offers both open stock decals as well as Custom Decals, using our own original designs as well as designs provided by our customers. We specialize in ceramic, glass, textile and helmet transfer decals.
    SHELPRINTS India is a true testimony to the coming together of technology and commitment, producing world-class quality and reliability at very competitive prices. It is this advantage that helps us to work with customers all over the world.

    CERAMIC DECALS

    We are India's leading suppliers in quality screen-printed ceramic decals. Ceramic decals are used to apply printed designs to ceramic objects such as tableware, tiles, ornaments and sanitary ware. These are fired at temperatures of up to 1200 degrees.

    Read More

    GLASS DECALS

    We are India's leading suppliers in high quality screen-printed glass decals. Most of the Indian glass-manufacturing units buy decals from SHELPRINTS India for glass decoration purposes. Decals for fragrance bottles, spirit bottles and candleholders are fired at temperatures ranging from 200 to 600 degrees.

    Read More

    HELMET DECALS

    We currently supply to the entire top helmet manufacturing companies across India. We have an edge over the others as we have our own design studio to help customers for speedy work. We use 2 different types of decals, peelable and non-peelable.

    Read More

    TEXTILE DECALS

    In 2007, we started off in a joint venture with Technic Graphic, UK to make textile decals. For the last 2 years we have been on our own. We have a complete setup to produce textile decals by screen and litho. We import all raw materials from reputed manufacturers.We can also produce PVC free prints on screen and litho.
